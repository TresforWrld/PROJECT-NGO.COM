Our Mission

Grounded in faith.
Fuelled by justice.

HGY Mission — Holy Ground of Yahweh — was born from the conviction that faith without action is hollow. We stand in the gap for those stripped of their rights: the persecuted, the trafficked, the oppressed, and the forgotten — providing advocacy, legal support, and restoration.

Human Rights Advocacy
We amplify the voices of the weak before courts, governments, and international bodies — ensuring no case goes unheard.
Urgent Crisis Response
Rapid-response teams deployed to reach individuals and families in acute danger or displacement — because rights don't wait.
Community Restoration
Long-term rebuilding programmes that restore dignity, provide trauma counselling, and reintegrate survivors into society.

Where We're Going

Our goals & future endeavours

We are a young organisation with a bold mandate. Here is where we are headed — the milestones we are building toward and the change we intend to make.

2026 — 2027

Establish Operations in 5 Nations

Launch ground-level teams, local partnerships, and reporting networks in our five priority countries across East and Central Africa.

2027

Open Our First Legal Aid Clinic

A permanent, walk-in legal aid centre staffed by pro-bono lawyers and paralegals providing free services to victims of rights violations.

2027 — 2028

Launch 3 Safe Haven Locations

Establish certified safe houses offering emergency shelter, trauma care, and 90-day restoration programmes for survivors of abuse and trafficking.

2028

Train 500 Community Advocates

Build and certify a network of local community advocates trained in human rights law, documentation, and non-violent civic action.

2028 — 2029

Youth Rights Academy in 10 Schools

Bring our rights education curriculum into secondary schools, reaching over 10,000 young people with the tools to understand and defend their rights.

2030

UN Consultative Status

Attain ECOSOC consultative status with the United Nations, amplifying our advocacy to the highest international human rights platforms.

Our 2030 Vision

A world where no one suffers alone — and every violation of human dignity is met with swift, faithful action.

Rights for All, Everywhere
Expand to 18 nations with active human rights protection teams on the ground.
International Recognition
Become a recognised voice at global human rights forums, policy tables, and UN bodies.
Zero Cost to Victims
Sustain a fully donor-funded model so every service remains permanently free to those we serve.
